I am Hujie Wei, a Control Engineering graduate with an M.E. degree focused on the development and implementation of advanced control algorithms for autonomous unmanned systems. My core expertise lies in Adaptive Control, Sliding Mode Control, and Formation Control.

During my Master's studies, I successfully designed a double closed-loop control system, combining an **Adaptive Fast Nonsingular Terminal Sliding Mode (AFNTSM)** controller with a **Nonlinear Disturbance Observer (NDOB)**. This method effectively achieved high-precision trajectory tracking for a quadrotor UAV, specifically addressing external disturbances and actuator saturation while ensuring stability via the Lyapunov direct method.
