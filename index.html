I am **Hujie Wei**, a Control Engineering graduate with an M.E. degree focused on the development and implementation of advanced control algorithms for autonomous unmanned systems. My core expertise lies in **Nonlinear Control**, **Sliding Mode Control (SMC)**, and **Disturbance Rejection**.

During my Master's studies, I successfully designed a double closed-loop control system, combining an **Adaptive Fast Nonsingular Terminal Sliding Mode (AFNTSM)** controller with a **Nonlinear Disturbance Observer (NDOB)**. This method effectively achieved high-precision trajectory tracking for a quadrotor UAV, specifically addressing external disturbances and actuator saturation while ensuring stability via the Lyapunov direct method.

My current interests include expanding into **Multi-Agent Cooperative Control**, **Reinforcement Learning (RL)** for control optimization, and applying robust methods to complex dynamic systems.
